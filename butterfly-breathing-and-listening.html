🍃 Why we breathe

When you feel big feelings — worried, angry, excited, or nervous — your body speeds up: your heart beats faster and your thoughts race. Slow, gentle breathing sends your brain a quiet message: you are safe. Your heart slows, your muscles loosen, and your mind feels clear again.

  • 💜Calms worry and big feelings when they feel too strong
  • 🎯Helps your brain focus and pay attention
  • 🌍Cools down anger before it grows too big
  • 💤Helps your body settle so it is easier to fall asleep
  • ✨You can do it anywhere — at school, in bed, or on the bus — and no one even knows
Just like a caterpillar grows a little with every leaf, you grow calmer with every breath. Three short exercises, only a few breaths each.
